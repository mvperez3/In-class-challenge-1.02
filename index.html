Kali Uchis

Kali Uchis is an American singer and songwriter born on July 17, 1994. Her career dates back to 2012 with her debut mixtape “Drunken Babble,” and ever since she has started to win a few grammys. Kali Uchis is of Columbian descent and loves to sing in both in Spanish and English. She is very well known in Latina America as well as in the States. She has collabed with other famous artists such as Tyler the Creator, Gorillaz, Diplo, Major Lazer and Snoop Dog.
